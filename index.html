Our Story

Welcome to the Warmth of Senegal

At Teranga African Restaurant, we share the warmth, flavor, and spirit of Senegal — one plate at a time. Trained in Senegal and seasoned on Chicago's South Side, our chefs bring you dishes rooted in tradition, rich in West African spices, and prepared with love.

"Ku nekk ci ñuul, am na ci weex"In Wolof: "In every darkness, there is light" — and we believe good food is that light.

In Wolof, teranga means more than hospitality — it's the art of making every guest feel like family. From the smoky grill of dibi to the tangy comfort of yassa, every bite carries a story of home, culture, and connection.

We prepare and serve timeless, high-quality West African dishes in a family-friendly atmosphere. Welcome, eat well, and leave as family.
